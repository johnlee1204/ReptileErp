This is a demo of a barcode generating script.

The barcodes are dynamically generated, and the image location is printed above each image.

The first /x/ after /barcode/ is the type of barcode. Valid types are:

1 - BC_TYPE_CODE39
2 - BC_TYPE_INTER25
3 - BC_TYPE_STD25
4 - BC_TYPE_CODE93
5 - BC_TYPE_ROYMAIL4
6 - BC_TYPE_POSTNET
